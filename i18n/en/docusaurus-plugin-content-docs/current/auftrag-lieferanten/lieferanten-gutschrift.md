# Supplier credit note

How to create a supplier credit note.

**Video:** [Supplier credit note](https://youtu.be/tJLJ4UsYTuE)

## Steps

1. Record the supplier document as in [Record supplier document](/docs/auftrag-lieferanten/lieferantenbeleg-erfassen).
2. In the window menu choose **Document print** → **Credit note**.
3. Click **Screen** to complete the credit note.
