# Take over open supplier order

How to book received goods into the system (goods receipt invoice).

**Video:** [Take over open supplier order](https://youtu.be/cAOSbdeKaF0)

## Steps

1. In the menu bar click **[1] Open orders**. A window opens with all open supplier orders.
2. Double-click the **[2] row header** of the desired order. The full order is shown. Compare the supplier invoice with the displayed order.
3. **Tip:** Easiest check: compare article total **[1]** and net purchase total **[2]** with the invoice. If they differ, enter the actual delivered quantity in the **Quantity** column and save. If they match, under **Extras** **[3]** click **Release positions** **[4]**.
4. Click **Document print** **[1]** and choose **Invoice** **[2]**. In the dialog enter the invoice number under **External document number** **[3]** and confirm with **OK**. (You can change the invoice date if needed.)
5. Print the goods receipt invoice (**OK** **[1]**) or display it on **Screen** **[2]**.

Goods are now booked. To verify: field **[1]** should show **ZU** (goods receipt) and **[2]** **Completion** should be ticked.
