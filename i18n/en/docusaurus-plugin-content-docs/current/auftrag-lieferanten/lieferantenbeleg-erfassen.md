# Record supplier document

How to create a new supplier order and enter the line items.

**Video:** [Record supplier document](https://youtu.be/_wONgD0iakI)

## Steps

1. Go to **Supplier orders** → **Order suppliers**.
2. Click **New record** in the toolbar to create a new supplier order.
3. Enter the first letters of the supplier (e.g. "CO") and press **ENTER** or click **…** next to the Matchcode field. A list of matching suppliers is shown. (The list appears only when several suppliers match.)
4. Select the supplier with **ENTER** or double-click. The document is now assigned to that supplier.
5. Save with the **save icon** or **F10**.

### Add line items

6. In the line list toolbar click **New record**. A new line is created; the **RefNr** field is active.
7. In **RefNr** enter a supplier article number or barcode (or scan). Press **ENTER** or click **…**. If several matches appear, choose the correct article.
8. Price lookup runs and the purchase price is shown. Correct if needed (the price applies to the line’s unit, e.g. case).
9. Enter the **Quantity** and any free quantity.
10. Save the line with the **save icon** or **F10**.
11. Repeat steps 6–10 for all other items.
