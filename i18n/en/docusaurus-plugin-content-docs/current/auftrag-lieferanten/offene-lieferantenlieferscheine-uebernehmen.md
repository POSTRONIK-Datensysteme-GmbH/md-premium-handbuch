# Take over open supplier delivery notes

How to book received goods from open delivery notes into the system.

**Video:** [Take over open supplier delivery notes](https://youtu.be/BQmeCZ1I6VA)

## Steps

1. In the menu bar click **[1] Open delivery notes**. A window opens with all open supplier delivery notes.
2. Double-click the **[2] row header** of the desired delivery note. The full delivery note is shown. Compare the supplier invoice with the displayed delivery note.
3. **Tip:** Check article quantity **[1]** and net purchase total **[2]** against the invoice. If they differ, enter the actual delivered quantity in **Quantity** and save.
4. Click **Document print** **[1]** and choose **Invoice** **[2]**. In the dialog enter the invoice number under **External document number** **[3]** and confirm with **OK**. (You can change the invoice date if needed.)

Goods are now booked. To verify: field **[1]** should show **ZR** (invoice).
