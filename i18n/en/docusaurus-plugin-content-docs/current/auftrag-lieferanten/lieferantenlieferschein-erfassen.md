# Record supplier delivery note

How to create a supplier delivery note from a supplier document.

**Video:** [Record supplier delivery note](https://youtu.be/E23-q_FlHWk)

## Steps

1. Record the supplier document as in [Record supplier document](/docs/auftrag-lieferanten/lieferantenbeleg-erfassen).
2. Left-click the **Extras** field. In the menu choose **Release positions**.
3. Under **[1] Document print** click **[2] Supplier delivery note**. A window opens.
4. Enter the delivery note number under **[3] External document number** and confirm with **OK**. (You can change the delivery date if needed.)
5. To print: click **[1] OK**. If you do not want to print, display it once with **[2] Screen** (close with **X**).
