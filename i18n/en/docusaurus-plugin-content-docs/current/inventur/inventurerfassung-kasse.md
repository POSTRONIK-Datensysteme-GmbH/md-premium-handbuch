# Inventory capture at till

How to capture inventory at the till.

**Videos:** [Inventory mode on](https://youtu.be/-n_bCE5U3DM) · [Inventory mode off](https://youtu.be/gKop6hGWrzQ)

## Prerequisite

- A cashier must be logged in.

## Turn on inventory mode

1. Set the mode to **REG2** (press **REG**).
2. Enter **4070** and press **SUBTOTAL**. The till shows "TRAINING ON" or "INVENTORY ON".

## Capture articles

3. Process articles as for a sale:
   - Enter quantity, press **X**, scan article.
   - After at most **50 articles**: press **Subtotal** and **CASH** (close receipt).

## Turn off inventory mode

4. Enter **4071** and confirm with **SUBTOTAL**. The till shows "TRAINING OFF" or "INVENTORY OFF".
5. After inventory: set mode back to **REG1**.

## Next step in MD-Premium

About **10 minutes** after the last till capture, data is transferred to MD-Premium. You can then edit the captured articles under **Data maintenance / Inventory / Edit inventory articles** (see [Post inventory data](/docs/inventur/inventurdaten-verbuchen)).
