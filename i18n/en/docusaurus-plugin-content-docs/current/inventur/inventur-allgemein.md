# Inventory overview

Inventory is used to record current stock levels. For each item counted, the **target stock** is calculated and stored at the time of counting. **Target/actual comparison** identifies inventory differences for review.

## Partial and full inventory

- **Partial inventory:** Only a limited number of articles are counted. Partial inventory **cannot** identify articles that still show stock in the system but are no longer in the store.
- **Full inventory:** All articles in the store from tobacco and non-tobacco assortments are counted. After counting and recording, inventory data is completed with articles that were not counted but still have stock.

## Preparation

- **Till release** must have been performed in MD-Premium.
- All **delivery notes** for goods in the store must be taken over.
- All **newspaper delivery notes** must be taken over and **till release** completed.
- Before starting, ensure **all sales totals for the inventory day** are posted in MD-Premium. Run **OLAP – Journal report**, view **HGR by day**, and check the **total sales**.
