# Inventory capture with handheld

How to capture inventory with a handheld device and transfer data to MD-Premium.

**Video:** [Inventory capture with handheld](https://youtube.com/shorts/Hqnba0bwY7E)

## Create inventory header and count articles

1. In the main menu select **INVENTORY**. The list of open inventory headers is shown.
2. **New header:** Press the blue **NEW** key, enter the **shelf number (= zone)** to count in the dialog, and confirm with the yellow **OK** (or **ENTER**). Open the new header with **ENTER**.  
   **Or:** Use **UP** or **DOWN** to an existing header and press **ENTER**.
3. Scan inventory articles. Each scan increases the article’s count by 1. Or: after scan press **ENTER** and enter the count, confirm with **ENTER**.
4. **End zone:** Press the yellow **Save** key. You return to the list of open headers.
5. **Close header:** Go to the header and close it with the red **Close** key. Confirm with **OK**. **Only closed** headers are transferred to MD-Premium.

## Transfer data to MD-Premium

6. Place the device in the **cradle** (charging and data transfer dock).
7. Wait until **"Transfer data now?"** appears. Press the yellow **OK** (keep the device in the cradle).
8. Wait for the transfer to finish (confirmed by a beep and message).

Data is sent to the office PC and posted in MD-Premium within **10 minutes**. Counted articles then appear under **Edit inventory articles** (see [Post inventory data](/docs/inventur/inventurdaten-verbuchen)). The zone can be reopened afterwards.
