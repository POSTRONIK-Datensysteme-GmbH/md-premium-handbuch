# Newspaper reorders

Newspaper reorders are first recorded as a "neutral" supplier document (**AN** = enquiry) and after all order lines are entered converted into a newspaper order (**BS** = newspaper order) and sent to the wholesaler (e.g. by e-mail).

## Option 1: Reorder from delivery notes

1. Proceed as in [Pre-return from delivery notes](/docs/zeitungen/vorremission-aus-lieferscheinen).
2. In the **context menu** (right-click) on a delivery line choose **Add to enquiry (newspaper order)**.
3. Enter the **reorder quantity** in the quantity dialog.
4. The newspaper item is added to an open **enquiry** of the supplier. If none exists, a new one is created.

## Option 2: Reorder from article master

1. **Master Data** -> **Articles**. Find the newspaper as in [Search existing articles by description](/docs/stammdaten/bestehende-artikel-suchen-bezeichnung).
2. Open the **[1] Issues info** tab and find the issue.
3. **[2]** Right-click the issue -> **[3] Add to enquiry (newspaper order)**. Enter quantity and confirm with **OK**.

## Complete and send order

4. **Newspapers** -> **New enquiries**. All open supplier enquiries are shown.
5. **Double-click** the row header of the enquiry – the full order is shown.
6. Window menu **Document print** -> **Order**. In the dialog confirm the order date (e.g. today).
7. In the print dialog: **Print** on paper or **Export** (e-mail). For e-mail choose **Export**.
8. In the next dialog check the e-mail and click **Send**. Confirm with **OK** (the suggested e-mail address should be correct).
