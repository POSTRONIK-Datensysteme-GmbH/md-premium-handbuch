# Take over newspaper delivery notes

Newspaper delivery notes are imported automatically from the EH-2000 system several times a day. Taking them over mainly means confirming receipt by setting the **Completion** flag.

## Steps

1. Go to **Newspapers** → **New Z delivery notes**. The window shows all unconfirmed newspaper delivery notes with net totals.
2. Find the delivery notes by **net delivery total**.
3. **No delivery differences:** Tick **Completion** to confirm the delivery.
4. **With delivery differences:** Open the document by double-clicking the row header and handle differences as in [Record delivery differences](/docs/zeitungen/lieferdifferenzen-erfassen).
5. The last confirmed delivery note may need to be saved with **F10** (or the save icon). When you switch to another row, the previous one is saved automatically.
6. Delivery notes may stay open for several days (e.g. delayed delivery).
7. Repeat until all paper delivery notes actually received are confirmed.

## Till release

When **all** paper delivery notes are confirmed in MD-Premium, run **Till release** (in the **Tills** folder in the toolbar) so that till documents can be posted.

- Release may **only** be run when **all** delivery notes from **all** suppliers are present and confirmed.
- Select the current date and your branch number in **Warehouse**.
