# Record newspaper delivery differences

How to record delivery differences on a newspaper delivery note opened under [Take over newspaper delivery notes](/docs/zeitungen/zeitungs-lieferscheine-uebernehmen) by double-clicking the row header.

## Steps

1. Select the **[1] Articles** tab and find the line with the delivery differences.
2. Enter the difference in the **[2] Difference** column.

**Rule:**

- **Over-deliveries** are entered **positive** (no sign).
- **Short-deliveries** are entered **negative** (leading **minus**, e.g. `-5`).

*(Example: 5 Kronen-Zeitung short, 10 Kurier over.)*

3. When all differences are entered, click **Create difference document** in the order header.
4. Close this difference document and the delivery document. Mark the delivery note as taken over by setting **Completion**.

Difference documents are sent to the wholesaler automatically. After further confirmation the new document (ID = "ZD") opens automatically.
