# Newspaper reservation list

How to view all reservations in one list.

**Video:** [Newspaper reservation list](https://youtu.be/zhQWNf9jfNw)

## Steps

1. Go to **Customer orders** -> **Order customers**.
2. In the form toolbar click the **Selection** icon.
3. Click the **Index list** icon or press **F2**.

All reservations are displayed.
