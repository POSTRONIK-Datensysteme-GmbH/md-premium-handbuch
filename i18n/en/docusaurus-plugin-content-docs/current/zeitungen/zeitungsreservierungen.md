# Newspaper reservations

How to record newspaper reservations for customers.

**Video:** [Newspaper reservations](https://youtu.be/tbyTQFWc4b4)

## Steps

1. Go to **Customer orders** -> **Order customers**.
2. Click **New** in the form toolbar. A new customer document is created; the cursor is in **Matchcode**.
3. Enter the first letters of the customer (e.g. "MUSTERMANN") and press **ENTER** or click **...** next to Matchcode. A list of matching customers is shown. (List appears only with multiple matches.)
4. Select the customer with **ENTER** or double-click. Save with the **save icon** or **F10**.

### Add line items

5. In the line list toolbar click **New record**. The **RefNr** field is active.
6. In **RefNr** enter a newspaper item number or barcode (or scan). **ENTER** or **...** – if several matches, choose the correct article.
7. Enter the **Quantity** and any free quantity.
8. Save the line with the **save icon** in the toolbar.
9. Under **Document print** **[1]** click **Newspaper reservation list** **[2]**.
10. Print the reservation (**OK** **[1]**) or display on **Screen** **[2]**. Close with **X**.

## Important

After taking over newspaper delivery notes, **EH2000 newspaper reservations** must be run daily. If not in the quick-access bar: **Data maintenance** -> **EH2000** -> **EH2000 newspaper reservations**.
