# Search existing articles by barcode

How to find an article in the system by its barcode (EAN) or supplier article number and open the corresponding master record.

**Video:** [Search existing articles by barcode](https://youtu.be/l7bOeU96nCM)

## Steps

1. Open the article form: **Master Data** → **Articles**.
2. Click the **barcode icon** in the top-right corner of the window.
3. Enter the barcode or supplier article number, or scan the article with the scanner.
4. Press **OK**. The article is displayed.

If an empty record is shown, the article is not in the system. The reference number is unique per article.

## Note: UPC and EAN codes

Some products have a US **UPC code** (12 digits). The till system converts it to a European **EAN code** (13 digits) by adding a leading **0**. The barcode must be stored in this EAN form in the system. Example: `070330600065` becomes `0070330600065`.
