# Search existing articles by product group

How to find all articles in a given product group.

**Video:** [Search existing articles by product group](https://youtu.be/jBWbNb3AjcE)

## Steps

1. Open the article form: **Master Data** → **Articles**.
2. Click the **selection window icon** next to **WGR** and choose the **All** button in the new window.
3. Select the product group by **double-clicking** its row header.
4. Press the **search icon** or **F3** to start the search.
5. The system lists all articles in that group. Use the arrow keys or **List view** (**F2**) to browse.
6. Click a column header to sort.
7. **Double-click** a row header to open the article detail view.
8. Close the list with **X** or **ESC**.
