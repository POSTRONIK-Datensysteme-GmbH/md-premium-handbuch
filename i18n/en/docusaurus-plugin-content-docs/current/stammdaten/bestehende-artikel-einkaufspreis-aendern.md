# Change purchase price of existing article

How to change the purchase price of an existing article.

**Video:** [Change purchase price](https://youtu.be/sNBcAntVThI)

## Steps

1. Find the article as described in [Search existing articles by barcode](/docs/stammdaten/bestehende-artikel-suchen-barcode) (or by description/product group).
2. Click the **(1) Purchase** tab. The current purchase price is shown.
3. Select the old price in **(2) Amount** (net purchase price) and overwrite it.
4. Click the **(3) Save** icon or press **F10** to save.
