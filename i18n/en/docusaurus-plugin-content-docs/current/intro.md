# Welcome to the MD-Premium Manual

This manual helps you work with **MD-Premium Merchandise Management** from POStronik.

## Areas

- **[Master Data](/docs/stammdaten/)** – Create and search articles, change prices, print labels
- **[Supplier Orders](/docs/auftrag-lieferanten/)** – Orders, delivery notes, credit notes, online orders (e.g. Tobaccoland, Philip Morris, Moosmayr)
- **[Inventory](/docs/inventur/)** – General inventory, recording at the till or with MDE, posting, lists
- **[Newspapers](/docs/zeitungen/)** – Z delivery notes, delivery differences, returns, reservations, invoice control
- **[Customer Orders](/docs/auftrag-kunden/)** – Customer orders, invoices, shrinkage, vending machine transfers
- **[Cash Register (XMPS)](/docs/kassa/)** – XMPS till software: operation (sales, void, returns …) and closing (change fund, zero receipt, end-of-day)

## Note

Most guides include **videos** that show the process step by step. Use the search (above) or the navigation on the left to find a topic.
