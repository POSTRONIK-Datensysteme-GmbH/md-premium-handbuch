# Print customer invoices

How to print customer invoices (combined till receipt) or send them by e-mail.

## Steps

1. In the menu go to **Customer orders** -> **Order customers** to show the last edited customer document.
2. Choose **Selection** -> **KA documents** and filter by **Customer ID** and **Date**.
3. Enter the **Customer ID** (6 digits) and the **date range** and confirm with **OK**.

**Note Customer ID:** If the customer is credited at till 21, the customer ID is **200021**; for till 101 it is **200101**.

4. Choose **Document print** -> **Combined document print** -> **Combined till receipt**.
5. Print the combined receipt or send it by e-mail (if an e-mail address is stored) by clicking **Export**. (With Outlook an e-mail opens.)
