# Record shrinkage (theft, loss, spoilage)

How to record shrinkage in MD-Premium (theft, loss, spoilage) via a customer document with the internal customer.

## Steps

1. Go to **Customer orders** and choose **Order customers**.
2. Click **New** in the form toolbar. A new customer document is created and shown in an empty form.
3. Enter **INTERN** in the **Matchcode** field and confirm with **ENTER**.
4. Save the new customer document with the **save icon** or **F10**.

### Add line items

5. In the line list click **New record**. A new line is created; the **RefNr** field is active.
6. Enter the supplier order number or barcode in **RefNr** (or scan) and select the article from the list.
7. Enter the **Quantity** and save the line with the **save icon**.
8. Repeat steps 5–7 for all other lines.
9. Under **Document print** click **Other outbound**.

Under **All reports** -> **OLAP Other outbound** you can run a report on shrinkage.
