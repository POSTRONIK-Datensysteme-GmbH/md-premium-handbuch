# Vending machine warehouse transfer

How to transfer articles to a vending machine warehouse.

## Steps

1. Go to **Customer orders** -> **Order customers**. Click **New record** in the toolbar. A new customer document is created; the cursor is in **Matchcode**.
2. Click **...** next to **Matchcode**. Select the **vending machine** (customer) with **ENTER** or double-click.
3. Click **...** next to **Target warehouse**. Select the **vending machine warehouse** with **ENTER** or double-click.
4. Save the customer document with the **save icon** or **F10**.

### Add line items

5. In the line list toolbar click **New record**. The **RefNr** field is active.
6. In **RefNr** enter a supplier article number or barcode (or scan). If several matches, select the correct article.
7. Enter the **Quantity**.
8. Save the line with the **save icon** or **F10**. Enter all other lines the same way.
9. After all lines: Under **Extras** **[4]** click **Release positions** **[5]**.
10. Under **Document print** **[1]** click **Warehouse transfer** **[2]**. Confirm the dialog with **OK**.
